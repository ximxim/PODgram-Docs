# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** October 21, 2025

Welcome to PODgram.ai. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use our Discord bot and related services (collectively, the "**Services**").

By using PODgram.ai, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## üìã Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you use PODgram.ai, we collect:

- **Discord User Information**: Your Discord user ID, username, discriminator, and avatar
- **Server Information**: Discord server (guild) IDs and names where our bot is installed
- **Text Prompts**: Any text descriptions you provide for image generation
- **Command Inputs**: Parameters and settings you choose when using bot commands
- **Generated Content**: Images and artwork you create through our Services
- **Payment Information**: Billing and transaction data processed through Stripe (we do not store your credit card details)

### Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:

- **Usage Data**: Commands used, timestamps, feature utilization, and interaction patterns
- **Technical Data**: IP addresses (collected by Discord), device information, and API request logs
- **Credit Balance**: Your current credit balance and transaction history
- **Error Logs**: Technical information about errors or issues you encounter

# Welcome to PODgram.ai

PODgram.ai (pronounced "pod-gram") is a Discord-based AI tool that turns your creative ideas into stunning print-on-demand artwork, in a matter of seconds. PODgram.ai excels at creating captivating designs, realistic images, and professional product-ready artwork for t-shirts, mugs, posters and more.

With unique capabilities like background removal, image analysis, and vectorization, we aim to empower creators and help every user turn their imagination into sellable products. Think of it as having an AI-powered graphic designer right in your Discord server, ready to bring your ideas to life instantly and affordably.

**Have a question? Check if it's been covered in our [Frequently Asked Questions](./faq).**
